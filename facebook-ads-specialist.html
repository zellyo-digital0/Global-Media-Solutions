  • Health Insurance, Life Insurance, 401K with company match

ROLE

  • ​​This is a mid-level role. At least 1 year experience running Facebook Ads.
  • Must be a highly motivated, self-driven, and data-driven individual with a passion for paid media campaigns
  • You will be responsible for the day-to-day management of paid media campaigns, ensuring they are optimized at all times and meet agreed to KPIs, which may also include scaling plans. In addition, you will be expected to work with the team to help direct and monitor strategies and implement new adjustments with ad creative, copy, bidding, etc.
  • As a side expectation, we will require you to remain up-to-date with industry updates, including new opportunities from partners and social channels and presenting opportunities to line managers.
  • Must possess a strong time management skillset and the ability to identify daily priorities and pivot when needed
  • High attention to detail is required

ROLE OVERVIEW

This role will be responsible for running Facebook ads for our clients. The ideal candidate will have 1 - 3 years of experience running Facebook ad campaigns for local small businesses. The typical budgets we work with are $1,000-$2,000/month for each client. Experience is required. You must have a desire to run ad campaigns and some background in marketing.

RRESPONSIBILITIES

  • Campaign Management - Work across multiple media and PPC channels, including Facebook, Snapchat, or TikTok Ads for lead generation in the orthodontics and dental niche with a range of budgets and requirements.
  • Optimization - Creation & ongoing optimization of campaigns to meet client KPI’s.
  • Budget & Spend Management - Daily monitoring of spend, bids, and scaling to ensure clients are on track to hit budget plans for each month.
  • Strategy Identification - Identifying key areas of opportunity & improvement within accounts & working with the full team to support and create new strategies.
  • Innovation - Researching & keeping up to date with the latest products to market ideas and being proactive in discussing & implementing new innovative ideas with the team.
  • Keyword and Audience Research - Researching to find missed opportunities within client accounts and undertake them.
  • Online Tools - Using a range of online tools to support performance analysis (e.g., Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Ahrefs, Google Keyword Planner, etc.) and using a range of available day-to-day tools such as Agency Analytics and Google Workplace.
  • Reporting - Creation of monthly reports for clients to analyze performance against KPIs & previous performance.
  • Must have knowledge of the Orthodontic and Dental field or be willing to learn
  • Online Tools- Using a range of online tools such as ClickUp, GoHighLevel (CRM), Slack, Zoom, Gmail, G Suite and Google Docs

RESULTS EXPECTED

  • Run all ads for clients’ Facebook accounts
  • Clients’ ad accounts are consistently generating expected results
  • Report to Sr. team consistently on performance and budget pacing
  • Ensure the manager is aware of the team’s fulfillment capacity
  • Internal communication is responded to timely

SUMMARY

  • We are looking for entry to mid-level paid advertisers with a passion and a can-do attitude in everything they do. The ideal candidate would be data-driven and completely capable of managing multiple projects and prioritizing tasks for many clients at one time.
  • They must be able to keep up with a fast-paced team and high-performance team and have a strong desire to exceed KPIs and be passionate about HIP, our team, our partner, and our vision.
  • They must possess a positive and results-driven mindset.
  • They are ideally experienced in building, optimizing, and launching advertising campaigns across various platforms.
  • Can implement pixels and pixel events, set up custom audiences, LLA audiences, and apply the right creatives and targeting to extract ROI from those segments.
  • Understand all paid channel performance metrics and analyze data to assess the performance of a campaign and problem-solve where necessary.
  • Strong analytical and creative problem-solving skills.
  • Possess an analytical mindset that has experience tracking and working toward KPIs.
  • Experience in using tools for CRO like Google Analytics
